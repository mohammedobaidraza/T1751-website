Why T1751?

T1751 is more than a name. It is a tribute to the year 1751, the birth year of Tipu Sultan, a leader remembered for his courage, vision, and relentless pursuit of progress. He was known as the Tiger of Mysore, a symbol of resistance, innovation, and resilience in the face of overwhelming odds.

Tipu Sultan — The Innovator

Tipu Sultan (1751–1799) wasn’t just a formidable ruler; he was a pragmatic innovator. Under his leadership, Mysore standardized iron-cased rockets with dedicated rocket brigades, an early example of state-backed R&D and battlefield deployment. He modernized the army with specialized corps, foundries, and armories, and invested in logistics and fortifications around Srirangapatna. Beyond the military, he pushed economic and industrial experiments from silk sericulture and weaving centers to state-managed trade in strategic goods, and drove administrative standardization in coinage, weights, and records so the state could learn faster and scale what worked.

T1751 – Frequently Asked Questions

What happens at T1751?
A focused, founder-first program: weekly workshops, 1:1s with operators, office hours, GTM/fundraising prep, and a strong peer cohort. You graduate with clearer focus, real customer feedback, and an investor-ready story.
Where does the program happen? Do I need to move?
Remote-first with optional in-person meetups. You don’t need to relocate; we design around global time zones.
How do you choose which startups to fund?
Founder grit & speed, insight into a real problem, early customer signals (even tiny), and a credible path to learn fast. Warm intros aren’t required.
How much do you invest and on what terms?
A standard pre-seed check on a founder-friendly SAFE, with potential follow-on. Exact terms are shared at interview/offer stage and listed on the Apply page.
What’s the time commitment & when are batches?
Plan ~5–10 hrs/week for live sessions, mentor time, and customer work. Batch dates and calendars are posted on the Apply page.
Am I too early or too far along (already raised)?
We back everything from napkin-stage to post-seed if you’re still searching for product–market fit. Prior funding doesn’t disqualify you.
Do you fund solo or non-technical founders? Only software?
Yes to solo founders. Non-technical founders can apply, be ready to show how you’ll build (co-founder, early hire, no-code, contractors). We fund software and software-enabled businesses.
Do we need to incorporate before applying? What if we’re non-US?
No, apply first. If accepted, we’ll guide you on a clean, standard setup. Non-US companies are welcome; we’ll help you navigate a US entity if useful.
The deadline passed, should I apply late? When will I hear back?
Yes, submit. We review late apps as capacity allows and reply on a rolling basis. If not this batch, we’ll consider you for the next.
What can I use the investment for?
Customer discovery, product, hiring your first builders/contractors, and go-to-market experiments. Keep it focused on learning velocity and growth.
