Why T1751?

T1751 is more than a name. It is a tribute to the year 1751, the birth year of Tipu Sultan, a leader remembered for his courage, vision, and relentless pursuit of progress. He was known as the Tiger of Mysore, a symbol of resistance, innovation, and resilience in the face of overwhelming odds.

Our emblem, the Huma bird, comes from the same tradition of legacy and meaning. In history, the Huma is said to never land, always soaring in the skies, and wherever its shadow falls, it is believed to grant leadership and fortune.

For us, it represents the journey of every founder — to rise above limits, to embrace destiny, and to build for impact.

"Together, T1751 and the Huma bird embody the spirit of our mission: to help founders begin, persist, and grow. We are not just an incubator. We are a launchpad where ideas take flight, guided by discipline, community, and vision."

Our Mission

T1751 is a launchpad for founders inspired by Tipu Sultan's legacy. Named after his birth year, our emblem — the Huma bird — symbolizes leadership and destiny. We exist to help ideas take flight with structure, discipline, and community.

We believe that courage, vision, and resilience are the foundations of every successful venture. Just as the Tiger of Mysore never gave up in the face of adversity, we support founders who dare to dream big and persist through challenges.

Our mission is to guide first-time founders to lasting impact, ensuring that wherever our shadow falls, leadership and fortune follow.

What You Get

At T1751, we provide more than funding — we offer a complete ecosystem for first-time founders. Our comprehensive program includes seed capital, mentorship from seasoned entrepreneurs, and access to a network of industry leaders who understand the journey from idea to impact.

You'll join a community built on the principles of courage, persistence, and vision. Our alumni network spans across industries and continents, united by the shared experience of taking their first flight with T1751.

Like the Huma bird that soars endlessly, we believe in supporting founders for the long journey ahead — from their first step to lifetime leadership.

1) What happens at T1751?
A focused, founder-first program: weekly workshops, 1:1s with operators, office hours, GTM/fundraising prep, and a strong peer cohort. You graduate with clearer focus, real customer feedback, and an investor-ready story.
2) Where does the program happen? Do I need to move?
Remote-first with optional in-person meetups. You don't need to relocate; we design around global time zones.
3) How do you choose which startups to fund?
Founder grit & speed, insight into a real problem, early customer signals (even tiny), and a credible path to learn fast. Warm intros aren't required.
4) How much do you invest and on what terms?
A standard pre-seed check on a founder-friendly SAFE, with potential follow-on. Exact terms are shared at interview/offer stage and listed on the Apply page.
5) What's the time commitment & when are batches?
Plan ~5–10 hrs/week for live sessions, mentor time, and customer work. Batch dates and calendars are posted on the Apply page.

Should I apply?

6) Am I too early—or too far along (already raised)?
We back everything from napkin-stage to post-seed if you're still searching for product-market fit. Prior funding doesn't disqualify you.
7) Do you fund solo or non-technical founders? Only software?
Yes to solo founders. Non-technical founders can apply—be ready to show how you'll build (co-founder, early hire, no-code, contractors). We fund software and software-enabled businesses.
